CALL OF DUTY: MODERN WARFARE III
SEASON 3 PATCH NOTES

Here you can find the latest news and patch notes for
Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 3!
Stay tuned for upcoming events and patches that
the game has to offer.

Season 3 Reloaded Has Landed! Check The Updates Below.

    Grime (6v6):


  • Take to the derelict streets of London in an all-new, medium-sized map that supports all varieties of gameplay.

    • Checkpoint (6v6):


  • Deploy to a familiar locale as the Stronghold portion of Rebirth Island has been modified to support 6v6 Multiplayer gameplay. Developed in partnership with our friends at Beenox.

    • New Season Weapon:


  • BAL-27 Assault Rifle: A bullpup prototype weapon designed to increase fire rate over time while the trigger is squeezed. The first 4 shots are slower to fire but highly accurate.
    Unlock it via the classified sector in the Season 3 Battle Pass.

    • New Game Modes Available:


  • Escort: In a game of strength and endurance, each team gets a turn to escort a MAW to its destination as the opposing team tries to stop it at all costs.
    Minefield: Watch your step! Deadly mines are deployed at the death site of players, triggered by the presence of the fallen players team.

    • New Events:


  • In addition to limited-time Double XP and the ongoing Vortex: Vi.Rus Mainframe events, the Call of Duty Endowment has a two-week event on the horizon.

New Maps and Weapons added into Season 3 Multiplayer:

    Das Haus (6v6):


  • h3A chaotic classic returns from Call of Duty: Vanguard (2021) with a fresh coat of paint.

    • Skidgrow (6v6):


  • h3An overgrown, limited-time variant of Skidrow.

    • Airborne (6v6):


  • h3A pestilence-ridden, limited-time variant of Terminal.

    • SOA Subverter (Battle Rifle):


    Chambered in 7.62, this hard-hitting rifle dominates at mid to longer ranges thanks to a moderate rate of fire and predictable recoil.

    Soulrender (Melee):


  • A ceremonial blade capable of razor-sharp cuts and deadly melee action in close-quarters combat.

Season 3 Mid-Season Patch Notes:

    UIX Fixes:


  • Increased the number of allowed favorite Attachments from 10 to 30.

  • Weekly Challenges that reward an Aftermarket Part will now display the base Weapon in the information modal.

  • Added Locked and Unlocked filter options to Calling Cards in the Customization menu.

    • Bug Fixes:


  • Unlocked Calling Cards that required Challenge completion once again indicate the fulfilled criteria.

  • Resolved an issue causing the Customization menu to not appear properly on ultrawide displays.

  • Addressed an exploit allowing multiple Attachments to be equipped to a single slot on a Weapon in the Gunsmith.

  • Pros and Cons are no longer hidden when viewing locked Attachments.
